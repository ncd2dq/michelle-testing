Who is Volante?

Volante is a Boston based company of women founded and directed by Michelle Garcia. It has happily established a repuation as a community of women who seek to make a difference in gender equality through the arts. As a salsa fusion company, Volante constantly does its best to think outside of the box.

During its debut year, Volante's performance team has started to travel and to this day continues to grow its success both in and out of Boston.

Volante hopes to be a constant reminder of what empowered women artists look like.

Michelle Garcia was born and raised in Ann Arbor, MI where she was classicly trained at Dance Theatre Studio beginning when she was 5 years old. Michelle joined a collegiate hip hop team while still in high school and went on to train in latin dances after a study abroad program sparked her love for salsa. She briefly trained under Laura Geidys at dance revolution and moved to the east coast upon graduation from the University of Michigan to persue a dance career.

Michelle then trained under the leadership of Joel Masocote before founding her own women's company, Volante. She now serves both as a director and solo artist and is currently expanding her full time dance career where she travels to perform, teach and choreograph. Both Michelle and Volante are based in Boston where she plans to stay as she grows as a solo artist and director - fighting for equality and kindness on all dance floors.
