Where do auditions take place?

Rumba Y Timbal Dance Studio: 7 Temple ST. Cambridge, MA

What time are auditions?

17:00pm - 9:00pm - Please arrive at 6:30pm to prepare!

What should I bring?

Appropriate workout clothes - Water Bottle - Salsa Shoes

Who are the auditions for?

Everyone! Volante will only be having one audition this year which means wether you would like to try out for the performance team or the training team you will all be welcome to this auditoin! Men, women, follows, leads are all encouraged!

How much do auditions cost and how can I pay?

$5 fee to audition: You may pay through Venmo, Zelle, Cash

What can I expect at auditions?

Auditions are very fast-paced but not to worry, we always have fun! We will start out with a warm up and move up to drills / technique. The second hour we will be learning combination and have a quick cool-down at the end.

When will I find out about my placement?

One week after auditions - by Monday, September 2nd
