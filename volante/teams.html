Volante Teams



Performance teams:

Volante has two different technique teams that combine into one cohesive performance team! This way, we can break down levels appropriately during technique every week and we can inspire each other by learning side by side an dsharing the stage all together! Our performance seasons last for 11 months out of the year from September to August and about 5 of those months we are performing both in and out of Boston!

Training Teams:

Our training teams are also broken down into two different levels. We heavily focus on basics and fundamentals throughout different units of each session and our training team sessions last for 2 months each. For our higher level training team we have a student performance option that is a 6 month training opportunity with local performance at the end of the season to show our work!

Volante hopes to be a constant reminder of what empowered women artists look like.
